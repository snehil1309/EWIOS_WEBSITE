About Us

Empowering Women Through Innovation

The Exposition of Women Innovation and its Outreach for Sustainability (EWIOS), hosted by Prasansa Foundation, celebrates women’s contributions to science, technology, and sustainability in India. Held November 5–8, 2025, EWIOS empowers women aged 18–35 to showcase innovations in sustainable fashion and eco-friendly solutions. Led by (Mrs) Kanak Lata and Shri S.K. Sinha, it fosters collaboration, offers certificates, awards, and startup support, enabling participants to transform ideas into impactful solutions.
